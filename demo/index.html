How to play Chinese Checkers:
  • Objective: move all 10 marbles to the opposite corner!
  • On your turn: a single "step" or many "hops."
  • Step: move one hexagon.
  • Hop: jump over a neighboring marble.
  • Hop as many times as you can in one turn!
  • Hop over anyone's marbles, even your own.
  • Hopping is harmless; marbles are never captured.
  • No hop-step-hop, it's one or the other.
  • Get every marble into the opposite corner to win!
Controls:
  • Click a piece to select
  • Click a different piece to change selection
  • Click again to deselect
  • Click destination to move
  • When done hopping, click piece to end turn
